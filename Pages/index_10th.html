After Class 10th

2 Years

Intermediate

Students who have completed class 10 must plan for the next stage, which is choosing a course after Class 10th. This requires a bit of thought and research as courses after 10th include Science, Arts and Commerce courses.

2 Years

ITI

ITI- Industrial Training Institute is a Technical course one can join after successful completion of Matriculation.ITI courses are available in different fields, and candidates can pick these based on their interests. ITI is a good job oriented Technical course.
3 Years

Polytechnic

Polytechnic is a diploma or vocational course in which an institute focuses on delivering technical education. Generally, polytechnic courses are a three year programme after completion of which candidates obtain a certificate.
3 Years

Paramedical

Paramedical courses refer to educational programs that train individuals to provide medical support services to patients and assist medical professionals in diagnosis, treatment, and therapy.
0.5-1 Year

Short Term Courses

short-term courses can be an excellent option to consider. There are plenty of 3 to 6-month courses available that can help you acquire valuable skills and knowledge in your desired field.

Government Job

Many government agencies provide excellent chances for jobs after 10th class. SSC, the railways, banking, and the police all provide a variety of government jobs after 10th.
